Background and History

Tim Berners-Lee was born July 8th, 1955 in London. He was the eldest of 4 and both of his parents were computer scientist. He grew up as a train enthusiast and learned about electronics while tinkering with model trains. After attending primary school at Sheen Mount Primary School, he went on to attend south-west London's Emmanuel School. He would later attend The Queen's College, Oxford from 1973 - 1976 where he built his first computer out of an old broken TV set.

From there, he would work for CERN in the UK and begin his career in computer science.
