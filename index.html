Home Page

This website is designed to help teach people about Tim Berners-Lee and his efforts in creating the World Wide Web. This is designed as a breif telling of the early days of the internet and some of Berners-Lee's motivations in creating an interlinked system of pages and information. Please use the navigational tabs at the top of each page to help learn a little more about the man that made this website even possible, Sir Tim Berners-Lee.
